about: "As a patient and carer you are in receipt of a lot of information from different
  providers, both within healthcare and beyond. This tool helps ease the burden on
  individuals, reducing the need for them to manage their documents physically and
  providing them an easy and automated way to manage the information. It includes
  optional 'passport' information - either input by the user or generated from information
  in documents added to the system.\n\nThe system includes automated reading of documents
  to record document name, date generated, specialism, location and other useful categorisation.
  It improves on the existing lack of information about unstructured data in the GP
  health record, where documents are rarely named, and dates refer to when they were
  added to the record rather than when they were produced. Secure sharing allows people
  to send time-limited links to professionals - so that relevant information can be
  reviewed once when those professionals do not have access to the information. \n\nThe
  vision for the future state of this is that its functionality exists within existing
  national products, like the NHS App. And that existing flaws in data sharing, integrations
  and barriers, challenges and limitations with NHS proxy access can be removed. \n"
