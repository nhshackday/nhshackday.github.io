about: 'Find My Jabs is a simple web tool designed to help people check their vaccination
  status. We built our demo with a focus on students preparing for university check
  their vaccination status and understand what they might be missing. Many people
  don’t know their vaccination history, how to access their NHS record, or what vaccines
  are recommended for their age, especially if they’ve moved around or are new to
  the UK.


  The tool guides users through a step-by-step journey. They can enter their NHS number
  to access their record, or, if they don’t have one, they’re directed to find it
  or register with a GP. Once they’ve accessed their record, they can confirm whether
  it looks complete, upload any additional information, or get guidance on where to
  find missing records. Their vaccination history is then compared against national
  guidelines, and the tool highlights any gaps and what they may need to catch up
  on. It also signposts users to their GP for further advice and next steps.


  Overall, the aim is to make a confusing and fragmented process much easier to navigate,
  and to help people feel more confident that they are up to date on their vaccinations.'
