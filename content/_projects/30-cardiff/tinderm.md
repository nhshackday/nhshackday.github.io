  and blind crosscheck for safety.

about: "Tinderm is a web-based swipe interface for dermatologists to rapidly review
  skin lesion images. Clinicians review batches of 20 at a time, swiping right for
  benign, left for biopsy, or up to share with a colleague. Each image can be tapped
  to view a context card with demographics, comorbidities, lesion site, family history,
  and previous skin cancers. At the end of each batch, users can review and edit their
  decisions before confirming. \n\nTo enhance safety, Tinderm includes a blind crosscheck
  system, where selected images from one clinician’s batch are silently shown in another
  clinician’s batch, ensuring high-risk or uncertain cases are double-reviewed without
  slowing workflow. The platform also uses weighted repetition to surface underrepresented
  groups and complex cases, creating a richer, safer, and more equitable review process."
