summary: "How to find the way to my hospital appointment. \nOften patient information
  to the specific department includes a map but when I get there how do I know where
  I am going in a busy noisy environment or where it's quiet and there is no-one around."

about: "We asked for ideas on how this would be possible using digital technology
  ( phone ) that most people have access to. \nUsing existing online information already
  provided by a local NHS hospital and Therapies department we brought this information
  together in one page, and included a video walkthrough showing the way. \nAccessible
  apps already exist that show available access to e.g. wheelchair users, so there
  is potential to link with this service too. \nThis video can now be made bespoke
  for out departments and updated if our location changes, it can be rolled out to
  other services. We can also include this in our onboarding induction material for
  new staff.\n"
